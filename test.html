Business Operations – Account Integrity Specialist

Ottawa

The Shopify Business Operations team is looking to expand our capabilities and we are looking for a unique individual to join the team and contribute to a more in-depth understanding of account integrity and compliance. This would involve escalated support contacts relating to disputes surrounding merchant account ownership, access, and account integrity for the over 300,000 merchants using the Shopify platform.

We are looking for someone to join our team, learn about our processes, and work with our merchants to resolve account access related issues. The end goal is an improved merchant experience for legitimate account holders and a sustainable operational model for our team’s areas of responsibility.

Your responsibilities would include:

  • Verifying identities of Shopify merchants through light touch verification methods
  • Learning the basics of risk analysis
  • Handling escalations around Shopify accounts
  • Working with the Shopify Business Operations team to resolve issues with; Account/Business ownership, Forgotten or mistyped email addresses, Unauthorized account activity, Triaging and investigating Compromised Accounts

Our ideal candidate would:

  • Be very detail-oriented and methodicalHave great communication skills, especially when working with multiples levels of organizationBe interested in learning more about risk/underwriting
  • Be comfortable in high-stress situations and remain tactfulBe mindful while handling confidential information
  • Be able to learn processes and teach them to other members of the support team
  • Be willing to work extra hours - not the typical 9-5 office job
  • Have an understanding of how to make data-informed decisions
  • Have a creative approach to problem solving
  • Have an understanding of profile and case building
  • Be able to accurately and efficiently identify and address account related issues
  • Have an understanding of ecommerce
  • Be able to deliver feedback and coaching to team members
